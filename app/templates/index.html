VirtuLink is a next-gen virtual hub designed for Infopro Digital. Tailored to foster seamless internal communication, team synergy, and HR management, it blends immersive design with interactive tools. Operating across 21 countries and multiple industries, this digital village enhances—not replaces—existing platforms, offering a unified, engaging employee experience.
