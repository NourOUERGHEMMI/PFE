Inside Virtulink

Discover how we're redefining
collaboration at Infopro Digital

VirtuLink is a next-gen virtual hub designed for Infopro Digital. Tailored to foster seamless internal communication, team synergy, and HR management, it blends immersive design with interactive tools. Operating across 21 countries and multiple industries, this digital village enhances—not replaces—existing platforms, offering a unified, engaging employee experience.

Why VirtuLink ?

Modern enterprises face growing complexity

From fragmented communication to disconnected teams and inefficient HR tools, the digital workplace can often feel chaotic. VirtuLink was designed to fix that.

Easy Communication
A centralized, immersive space that brings all employees together—no matter their location.
Smooth Collaboration
Virtual offices and meeting spaces that simulate real-life interactions and foster collaboration.
HR Management
A modern, interactive layer that enhances existing HR tools and makes the employee experience more human.
Global Connection
A virtual village that adapts to diverse teams and keeps everyone aligned, across 21 countries.
